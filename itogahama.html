Itogahama

History and Background

Itogahama Park is also a well-loved park in Hiji, Oita Prefecture. It is famous for its beautiful beach along the Seto Inland Sea. The park was developed to offer a relaxing and fun place for both locals and tourists. Over the years, the local government and community have worked together to improve the park's facilities. These include picnic areas, playgrounds, and barbecue spots. The park is also a place for local events and festivals, especially in the summer. These events help bring the community together and attract visitors. The park's clean environment and natural beauty are maintained through ongoing conservation efforts. Itogahama Park is an important part of Hiji, providing a wonderful space for recreation and community activities.

Why we should go there

Visiting Itogahama Park is a wonderful experience for everyone. The park has a beautiful, clean beach with calm waters, perfect for swimming and sunbathing. It is also popular for stand-up paddleboarding (SUP), which is fun and easy to learn in the gentle waves. The park has many facilities like camp sites, barbecue spots, and playgrounds, making it a great place for families. Throughout the year, Itogahama Park hosts many events and festivals, offering a chance to enjoy local culture and community activities. Spending time here is good for your health, as you can enjoy fresh air and physical activities. The park is well-kept and supports environmental conservation, ensuring a clean and natural space for visitors. By visiting Itogahama Park, you also help support the local economy. Whether you want to relax by the beach, try out SUP, or join in on community events, Itogahama Park has something for everyone.
